Here are some Regular Expressions to try (just cut and paste them)


\bt[a-z]+\b
- this will give positive if any word begins with t and is surrounded by blank spaces \b for blank, t for first letter, [a-z]+ for multiple alpha characters.

\S
- Is not blank, lower case \s means matches character or whitespace, Caps \S means opposite of \s, thus no characters or whitespace.

^\s*\d+\s*$
- Is all digits ^ means negate the next item in expression which is \s, which is characters or whitespace, * turns off the ^, \d+ means one or more digits, \s*$ means must match pattern with characters until end of string.
